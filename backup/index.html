Split-Aware Learning for IoT Intrusion Detection under Temporal Domain Shift

Anh-Minh Phan, and collaborators

Under Review 2025

Random train–test splits remain common in RPL-IoT intrusion detection but mainly measure matched-distribution recovery rather than forward-in-time robustness. We present a split-aware learning framework separating random and chronological protocols. Adaptive Temporal Alignment (ATA) achieves strong improvements under temporal domain shift (+6.58 macro-F1 on ROUT-04), demonstrating mixture-of-experts for robust IoT security in realistic deployment settings.

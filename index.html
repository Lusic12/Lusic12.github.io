Hi, I'm Anh-Minh! 👋
I am a researcher focusing on Computer Vision (Segmentation, Detection, Tracking).
My advisors are Postdoc. Mã Linh Văn and Prof. Nhật Hồ.

"I study Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) for adaptive learning from heterogeneous views."
Many real-world AI problems involve multiple perspectives of the same scene or event. My research explores how Mixture-of-Experts can model this diversity by learning how to route inputs adaptively.
    • Multi-view and cross-view perception: Expert routing for detection and tracking across multiple camera viewpoints.
    • Multi-modal expert fusion: Integrating heterogeneous sensors.
    • Model generalization under data bias: Enhancing robustness against temporal shifts and biased distributions via specialized expert routing.
